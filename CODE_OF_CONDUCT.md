# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders in the CURSE community pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and receiving constructive feedback
* Accepting constructive feedback
* Showing empathy towards other people

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional practice.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers through a private GitHub issue (marked as a draft). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is proportional to the violation.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is representing the community in public spaces.
